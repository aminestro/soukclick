# Product Adding Guide

## Goal

Adding a new product should require product data and media only. The product page template, offers, reviews, FAQs, and CRO sections should be reusable.

## Product Checklist

For every product, prepare:

- Product slug.
- Darija product name.
- Short headline.
- Emotional subheadline.
- Product category.
- Main benefits.
- Problem/solution sections.
- Product media.
- Reviews.
- FAQ.
- Cross-sells.
- Frequently bought together.
- Upsell candidate.

## Required Product Fields

- `slug`
- `name`
- `darija_name`
- `headline`
- `subheadline`
- `description`
- `category`
- `base_price`
- `offers`
- `media`
- `benefits`
- `reviews`
- `faqs`

## Default Offers

Unless product-specific pricing is approved:

- 1 piece: 199 MAD.
- 2 pieces: 279 MAD.
- 3 pieces: 349 MAD.

## Copywriting Process

1. Identify the real daily pain.
2. Write the benefit in Moroccan Darija.
3. Keep claims realistic.
4. Add COD reassurance.
5. Add usage scenarios.

## Media Guidelines

Until real assets are available:

- Use clean generated placeholders.
- Make images product-specific, not abstract.
- Include lifestyle usage.
- Include close-up detail.
- Include before/after style visuals where useful.

Later real media should include:

- Moroccan home context.
- Vertical videos for TikTok/Reels.
- Simple demonstrations.
- Hands using the product.

## Product Launch QA

- Product page loads on mobile.
- Offer selector works.
- Cart drawer receives correct product and price.
- Checkout creates correct order.
- Tracking events include product data.
- Product schema validates.
- Images have alt text.
