# Cart Drawer Flow

## Trigger

When the customer clicks "زيدي للسلة" or "طلبي دابا", the cart drawer opens automatically.

## Drawer Goals

- Confirm that the product was added.
- Push the customer toward checkout.
- Increase AOV with cross-sells.
- Reinforce COD trust.
- Reduce abandonment.

## Required Drawer Elements

- Product list with quantity controls.
- Selected offer summary.
- Subtotal.
- Delivery reassurance.
- COD reassurance.
- Free shipping threshold progress bar.
- Recommended products.
- Scarcity or urgency message.
- Checkout CTA.

## Free Shipping Threshold

If free shipping is enabled, use progress copy:

- زيدي غير X درهم واستافدي من توصيل مجاني
- وصلتي للتوصيل المجاني

If not enabled yet, keep the architecture but hide the component with config.

## Cross-Sell Rules

Show 2-3 products max.

Prioritize:

- Same room use case.
- Complementary organization product.
- Product with simple low-friction value.

## Checkout CTA

Primary text:

كملي الطلب

Supporting text:

الأداء حتى توصلك السلعة

## Drawer Behavior

- Opens from the side on desktop.
- Opens as bottom sheet or full-height drawer on mobile.
- Checkout CTA sticky inside drawer.
- Escape key closes on desktop.
- Cart icon displays quantity.
