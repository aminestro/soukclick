# Product Page Structure

## Goal

The product page is the main conversion engine. It must work for cold Moroccan TikTok/Facebook traffic and be reusable for every future product.

## Page Order

1. Sticky header.
2. Product hero.
3. Trust strip.
4. Problem section.
5. Solution section.
6. Benefits section.
7. TikTok/Reels style UGC gallery.
8. Before/after section.
9. Offer reminder.
10. Reviews and testimonials.
11. Frequently bought together.
12. Cross-sells.
13. FAQ.
14. Delivery, COD, guarantee.
15. Final CTA.
16. Sticky mobile CTA.

## Product Hero

Required elements:

- Product gallery.
- Product title.
- Star rating.
- Review count.
- Emotional subheadline.
- Scarcity badge.
- Offer selector.
- Add-to-cart button.
- COD reassurance.
- Delivery hint.

## Section Alternation

On desktop, alternate content direction:

- Section 1: text right, image left.
- Section 2: image right, text left.
- Repeat.

On mobile:

- Image first when visual proof matters.
- Text first when problem framing matters.
- CTA always visible after major persuasion blocks.

## Reusable CRO Sections

Each product can define:

- `problemBlocks`
- `solutionBlocks`
- `benefits`
- `beforeAfter`
- `ugcItems`
- `reviews`
- `faqs`
- `crossSells`
- `frequentlyBoughtTogether`

## Product-Specific Page Angles

### Vegetable Chopper

- Save time in kitchen preparation.
- Less mess.
- Easier Ramadan and family cooking.
- Safer and faster chopping.

### Shoe Rack

- Cleaner entrance.
- More space.
- Better organization for families.
- Apartment-friendly.

### Indoor Coat Rack

- Organize clothes, coats, bags, and daily items.
- Useful for bedrooms and entryways.
- Reduces clutter.

### Washing Machine Organizer

- Uses vertical space above washing machine.
- Organizes detergents, towels, cleaning products.
- Ideal for small balconies and laundry corners.

## Product Schema Needs

Every product page must output structured data:

- Product name.
- Description.
- Images.
- Price.
- Currency MAD.
- Availability.
- Aggregate rating.
- Reviews when available.
