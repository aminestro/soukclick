# Scaling Guide For Future Products

## Scaling Principle

Souk Click should scale by adding structured product data, not by copying pages.

## Product Expansion Categories

- Kitchen helpers.
- Home organization.
- Laundry organization.
- Entryway organization.
- Bathroom storage.
- Cleaning tools.
- Small smart home gadgets.
- Practical family products.

## Before Adding A Product

Ask:

- Does it solve a clear daily problem?
- Can it be demonstrated in 3 seconds on TikTok?
- Does it fit Moroccan homes?
- Can it support bundles?
- Is it easy to explain in Darija?
- Is delivery practical?

## CRO Reuse

Every new product should use:

- Same offer selector.
- Same checkout flow.
- Same trust sections.
- Same review system.
- Same FAQ system.
- Same related products system.

## Operational Scaling

Future needs:

- Admin dashboard.
- Inventory management.
- Confirmation agent workflow.
- Delivery company integration.
- Automated WhatsApp confirmation.
- A/B testing.

## Data Discipline

Keep clean records for:

- Products.
- Offers.
- Orders.
- Campaigns.
- Confirmation statuses.
- Delivery statuses.
