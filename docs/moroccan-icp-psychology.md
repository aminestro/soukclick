# Moroccan ICP Psychology

## Primary ICP

Moroccan men and women aged 24-45, especially:

- Women interested in home organization.
- Married couples and families.
- Apartment owners or renters with limited space.
- People who buy from Facebook, TikTok, and Instagram ads.
- Customers who prefer cash on delivery because it feels safer.

## Main Buying Motivations

- "بغيت الدار تبان نقية ومنظمة."
- "بغيت نربح الوقت فالكوزينة."
- "بغيت حل عملي ماشي غير زواق."
- "بغيت نشري بلا ما نغامر بفلوسي."
- "بغيت شي حاجة تقدر تستعملها العائلة كاملة."

## Pain Points

- Small apartments and limited storage.
- Messy kitchens, entryways, balconies, and laundry corners.
- Products that look good in ads but are weak in real life.
- Fear of scams and fake stores.
- Fear that delivery will be slow or that the product will not match the ad.
- COD orders that require too many steps.

## Trust Triggers

- Cash on delivery clearly repeated.
- Moroccan phone number validation.
- Confirmation call or WhatsApp confirmation.
- Delivery city coverage.
- Realistic product claims.
- Reviews written in natural Moroccan Darija.
- Clear return policy.
- Product photos and videos that show real usage.

## Conversion Psychology

Moroccan COD customers often need enough reassurance to submit the order before they are fully committed. The website should:

- Make order placement feel low-risk.
- Confirm the product solves a daily problem.
- Make the bundle option feel like the intelligent choice.
- Reduce anxiety with repeated delivery and payment reassurance.
- Avoid asking for unnecessary fields.
- Make the thank-you flow feel official and clear.

## Message Angles By Segment

### Home Organization Buyers

- "كلشي فبلاصتو."
- "دار مرتبة بلا مجهود كبير."
- "حلول صغيرة كتبدل شكل الدار."

### Kitchen Buyers

- "وجد الخضرة بسرعة وبلا وسخ بزاف."
- "ربح الوقت فالكوزينة."
- "حل عملي لرمضان، العائلة، والتحضيرات اليومية."

### Family Buyers

- "منتوجات نافعة للدار كاملة."
- "راحة أكثر وتنظيم أكثر."
- "اختيارات ذكية بثمن مناسب."

### Male Buyers

- "حلول عملية للدار بلا صداع."
- "منتوج مفيد وسهل التركيب."
- "طلب سريع والدفع حتى توصلك السلعة."
