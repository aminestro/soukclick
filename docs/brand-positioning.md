# Brand Positioning

## Brand Name

Souk Click

Arabic subtitle:

سوق كليك

## Positioning Statement

Souk Click is a modern Moroccan brand that helps families organize their homes and improve daily life with practical, smart, and carefully selected products.

## Brand Promise

منتوجات عملية كتعاونك تنظمي دارك، تربحي الوقت، وتعيشي براحة أكثر.

## What The Brand Must Feel Like

- Clean and modern.
- Moroccan, but not folkloric or old-fashioned.
- Premium and trustworthy.
- Practical and conversion-focused.
- Warm, emotional, and family-aware.
- Serious enough for parents and homeowners.
- Simple enough for TikTok traffic to understand quickly.

## What To Avoid

- AliExpress-style visuals.
- Overloaded discount banners.
- Fake scarcity that feels desperate.
- Neon colors or chaotic badges.
- Generic English-only ecommerce copy.
- Cheap dropshipping wording like "hot sale", "limited crazy deal", or "buy now before gone".

## Visual Signals

- Deep red circular logo mark using `S` or `SC`.
- Black and white foundation.
- Light gray section backgrounds.
- Premium product photography placeholders until real Moroccan content is available.
- Small Moroccan details through copy, city names, payment reassurance, and delivery context.

## Brand Belief

Moroccan homes deserve practical products that look good, solve real daily problems, and arrive with a trustworthy cash-on-delivery experience.

## Core Message Pillars

- Practical solutions: every product must solve a visible daily problem.
- Home comfort: focus on calm, clean, organized living.
- Trust: COD, confirmation call, clear delivery, clear return policy.
- Moroccan relevance: Darija copy, Moroccan phone validation, Moroccan cities, local expectations.
- Smart value: bundles increase savings without making the brand feel cheap.
