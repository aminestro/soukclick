# CRO Strategy

## Conversion Principles

Souk Click must optimize for Moroccan COD behavior, not generic card-based ecommerce.

The page must quickly answer:

- What problem does this product solve?
- Can I trust this store?
- Can I pay after delivery?
- Is this useful for my home?
- Which offer should I choose?
- What happens after I submit my order?

## Funnel

1. TikTok/Facebook ad click.
2. Product page or homepage landing.
3. Fast benefit understanding in first viewport.
4. Offer selection.
5. Cart drawer opens after add-to-cart.
6. Checkout popup with 4 fields only.
7. Upsell popup for 10-15 seconds.
8. Thank-you page with order confirmation expectations.
9. Backend sends order to Google Sheets and tracking APIs.

## CRO Requirements

- Mobile-first layout.
- Sticky mobile CTA on product pages.
- Offer selector above fold.
- COD reassurance near every purchase action.
- Reviews and stars above fold.
- Scarcity near offer selector.
- Free shipping threshold in cart drawer.
- Cross-sells in cart drawer.
- Upsell after checkout, not before checkout.
- One primary CTA per section.

## Above The Fold Product Page

Must include:

- Product gallery with TikTok/Reels style vertical assets.
- Product title in Darija.
- Star rating and review count.
- Emotional subheadline.
- Offer selector.
- Scarcity badge.
- COD reassurance.
- Add-to-cart CTA.

## Trust Stack

Trust signals should appear repeatedly but tastefully:

- الأداء عند الاستلام.
- تأكيد الطلب عبر الهاتف أو واتساب.
- توصيل داخل المغرب.
- ضمان الاستبدال إذا وصل المنتوج متضرر.
- تقييمات زبناء مغاربة.

## AOV Strategy

- Bundle offers are the main AOV lever.
- Cart cross-sells add related products.
- Checkout upsell adds one discounted product at 99 MAD.
- "Frequently bought together" sections should be product-specific.

## Confirmation Rate Strategy

- Validate Moroccan phone numbers at checkout.
- Explain confirmation call on thank-you page.
- Include clear product names, quantities, city, and total.
- Send complete order data to Google Sheets for confirmation team.
- Flag suspicious orders before fulfillment.
